Kobe Jerome

Sports Marketing

My whole life has revolved around sports and relationships for as long as I can remember.I played basketball, baseball, football, and soccer at the youth level. I was even fortunate enough to play college basketball and compete at the Division 1 level. In fact, my entire immediate family played sports in college, so it is a part of who I am. Competitive nature is also a part of who I am, and I like that it translates in the workplace as well. When my college career finished up, I thought about ways I can still contribute in the sports world. That is when I decided to try Sports Marketing, and I fell in love with it. Being a Sports Marketer allows me to use my knowledge of all different sports and make money from it. I also love how sports marketing in today’s world has transitioned into a lot of social media promotions which I am very experienced in! My goal is to drive sales for sporting events, jersey sales, and to promote specific athletes as well. Not only do I have good knowledge of all sports, but I also have experience as an athlete at a high level as I previously mentioned. This gives me an advantage over many others in my field, since I know what kind of environment both athletes and fans enjoy being a part of. In addition, I have been doing hours and hours of research on the most effective ways to market brands. Social media such as Instagram, Twitter and TikTok seem to be the most useful applications from a marketing standpoint. Another effective marketing strategy is putting advertisements on Google based on the customers previous searches. As you may be able to tell, I am very invested into this business and I am excited to take the next step forward in becoming the next big Sports Marketer. Another thing that differentiates me from many others in this industry is that I am not here for the money. Sports may be a business at the end of the day but it also transforms lives, gives people hope, and creates bonds that will last a lifetime. I love being able to promote a product that I am passionate and fully invested in. Sports are bigger than individuals, bigger than companies, and bigger than business. I cannot wait to start creating that narrative again and start promoting the purity of sports at every level.
